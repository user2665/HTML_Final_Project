My VietNamese Favorite Dishes

      As a Vietnamese, I want to share my favorite dishes to promote our cuisine.

    1. Pho

    Pho is a Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat, usually beef.

    Pho is a popular food in Vietnam where it is served in households, street stalls, and restaurants countrywide.

    Find out more information about Pho

    2. Banh Mi

    Banh mi is a Vietnamese sandwich consisting of a Vietnamese single-serving baguette, which is split lengthwise and filled with various savory ingredients.

    Find out more information about Banh Mi

    3. Bun Cha

    Bun cha is a Vietnamese dish of grilled pork and noodle, which is thought to have originated from Hanoi, Vietnam.

    Find out more information about Bun Cha

    4. Hot Vit Lon

    Hot vit lon is a Vietnamese dish made from fertilized duck eggs, which are incubated for 19 to 21 days before being boiled or steamed.

    Find out more information about Hot Vit Lon
